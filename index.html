Welcome to RSDA 2022 Workshop!

RSDA 2022 will follow the hybrid conference model and it will allow online partecipation and remote presentation of accepted papers.

The 7th IEEE International Workshop on Resiliency, Security, Defenses and Attacks (RSDA 2022) is co-located with the 33rd Annual IEEE International Symposium on Software Reliability Engineering (ISSRE 2022) being held in Charlotte, North Carolina, USA, Oct 31 - Nov 3, 2022. The workshop follows its past successful editions held at both ISSRE and DSN (RSDA 2021,RSDA 2020,RSDA 2019, RSDA 2016, RSDA 2014, RSDA 2013).

RSDA Highlights:

  • RSDA 2022 is co-located with ISSRE, the top software systems reliability conference, and will feature exciting technical sessions and presentations;
  • The papers accepted at RSDA 2022 will be included in the ISSRE Supplemental Proceedings as well as in the ISSRE-W volume on IEEE Xplore.

RSDA 2022 aims to concentrate ideas and contributions from academic and industrial organizations addressing resiliency and security of computer systems through data analysis. RSDA aims to gather high-quality papers on data-driven methodologies, measurements from production systems, and analysis of large datasets.
RSDA is rapidly emerging as a forum to stimulate scientific research and discuss techniques, procedures and tools that are currently adopted to manage and analyze resiliency of systems under attack and defenses. The expected output of the workshop is establishing the agenda of future research activities in the field of reliability and security data analysis, with the ambition of creating concrete synergies between researchers and industrial bodies of different research communities addressed by RSDA.
