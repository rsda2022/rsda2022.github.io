Submission Instructions

The papers must be submitted electronically at the following link (you will be redirected to the EasyChair system):
https://easychair.org/conferences/?conf=rsda2016

The papers must be original with a maximum of 6 pages (including figures, tables and references). A paper has to include the title, the name and affiliation of each author, a 150-word abstract, and up to 4 keywords. Both research papers and industry experience reports are welcome.

All the submissions will be judged on novelty of the proposed problem and/or solution, technical relevance and practicality of presented ideas, relevance to the scope/topics of RSDA (RSDA CFP HERE), writing and presentation, accuracy and impact of the results.

Submission imply the willingness of at least one of the authors to register and attend the workshop, if the paper is accepted. The papers accepted at RSDA 2016 will be included in the DSN Supplemental Proceedings as well as in the DSN-W volume on IEEE Xplore.

Submissions must be written in English and adhere to the double column, IEEE conference style format. For your convenience templates are available at:

http://www.ieee.org/conferences_events/conferences/publishing/templates.html
